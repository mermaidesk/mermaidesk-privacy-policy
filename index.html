Privacy Policy for Mermaidesk: All-in-One STR Dashboard

Our commitment to your privacy
Mermaidesk is built to simplify the management of your short-term rental property, and your data privacy is a top priority. We believe in being transparent about what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used to provide you with a seamless and powerful service. We collect and process only the information necessary to provide the core functionality of the Mermaidesk dashboard. All data is securely handled and integrated with your Google tools for your control.

Information we collect and why we collect it

To function as your complete property management hub, Mermaidesk requests and stores certain information. This is all done with your consent and for the sole purpose of providing the dashboard's features.

1. Your personal and business information

  • What we collect: Your name, email address, and property details.
  • Why we collect it: This information is necessary to set up your Mermaidesk account, personalize your dashboard, and provide you with a unique, secure access point to your business hub.

2. Google account integration

  • What we collect: Permission to connect to your Google account and its associated services (e.g., Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Drive).
  • Why we collect it: This integration is the foundation of Mermaidesk's seamless experience. It allows you to:
    • Manage emails by syncing with your Gmail.
    • Keep track of bookings and events by integrating with your Google Calendar.
    • Store maintenance logs, forms, and photos securely in your Google Drive.
    • Access all your data through the powerful and familiar Google ecosystem.
  • Our approach to privacy: Mermaidesk never accesses or uses your personal Google data for any purpose other than providing the features you specifically use within the app. We do not sell, share, or misuse your Google data. All data remains under your direct control within your own Google account.

3. Booking and contact information

  • What we collect: Guest and contact details, booking dates, stay information, and guest communication history.
  • Why we collect it: This data is essential for the booking tracker, calendar, and contacts features. It allows you to efficiently manage reservations, communicate with guests, and maintain a historical record of your business interactions.

4. Financial data

  • What we collect: Records of income and expenses related to your rental property.
  • Why we collect it: This data powers your income/expense tracking feature. It enables you to monitor the financial performance of your rental business in one place.

5. Task and maintenance data

  • What we collect: Details of tasks, maintenance logs, and associated media (e.g., photos uploaded via Google Forms).
  • Why we collect it: This information is used to manage and track maintenance and task logs for your property. By integrating with Google Forms and Google Drive, the app provides a smooth workflow for you and your staff.

Who we share data with

Mermaidesk does not share your personal or business data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your data is your own. The only data sharing that occurs is through the Google integrations you authorize, which is managed entirely by you within your own Google account.

Your control over your data

At any time, you can manage your data by:

  • Accessing your data directly within your Google account (Drive, Calendar, etc.).
  • Disconnecting your Google account from Mermaidesk.
  • Requesting the deletion of your Mermaidesk account and any associated information.
